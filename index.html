Hi! I'm Chetan, an experimentalist working
on artificial gauge fields with ultracold strontium atoms

In Assoc. Prof. David Wilkowski's group, we create non-Abelian gauge fields
using atom-light interaction and simulate their effect on atomic wave packet in free space. By directly observing the dynamics of
the wave packet, we aim to extract geometrical and topological properties of the underlying Hamiltonian.
Visit our lab at Nanyang Technological University.
